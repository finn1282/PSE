EduVid AI is an educational platform that converts study materials (PDFs, slides, notes) into AI-generated video lessons presented by historical figures like Marie Curie, Albert Einstein, and Ada Lovelace.

## Problem Statement
Students struggle with text-heavy materials, low engagement, and limited access to inspiring educators.
## Our Solution
- Upload study materials (PDF, DOCX, PPTX)
- Select from diverse public figure presenters
- Receive AI-generated educational videos
- Learn through engaging, personalized content
## Target Users
- High school students
- University students
- Self-directed learners
- Anyone seeking accessible, engaging education
