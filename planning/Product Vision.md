# Product vision 
EduVid AI is expected to work on traditional learning resources and transform them into
interactive video lessons based on artificial intelligence. The platform will facilitate
Sustainable Development Goal (SDG) 4: Quality Education by ensuring that learning is
more accessible, personalized, and engaging among the students with various education
levels. As the pain points of student are often, information overload, impersonal education
contents, engagement decay and asynchronous assesibility. Most target users struggles with
dense, text-heavy slides. Short attention span due to lack of multi sensory features of a video
and time management, studying in their “own” time. Conventional forms of learning tend to
use rather stagnant sources of information, e.g., lecture presentations, books and written
sources. These formats do not necessarily help to attract the attention of students and support
other learning styles. EduVid AI is the solution to this weakness, as it allows users to upload
study resources, which are automatically transformed into AI-generated video lessons
delivered by a selection of prominent personalities.

The vision of the product is based on the following three objectives:
- Improving the learning activities using educational materials in a multimedia format.
- Encouraging self-directed learning by allowing the students to transform their own
resources into systematized lessons.
- Facilitating open and accessible learning by using AI-driven content creation and
presenters of diverse backgrounds. With these goals, EduVid AI will offer a new
educational platform that will enable learners to grasp complex concepts better and
have flexibility in their studies at the same time.
